Magic the Gathering Damage Calculator

How to use this tool

To calculate damage during combat use the two cards below to setup your Attacker and Blocker. A card's Strength value goes in the left box and Toughness value in the right. Select any abilities that are in play such as First Strike, Deathtouch, etc, and when you're ready click the Battle button!

The cards will show both who won the battle as well as the results for keeping track on your own counters if any. Lifelink if returned will be in a green box.
